The Best Movies According to Amrit

My top 3 movies of all-time

Kgf chapter 1

KGF Chapter 1 is a visually stunning Kannada film with Yash as a compelling anti-hero. Its gripping action and dramatic story overshadow minor pacing issues and underdeveloped characters.

Kill

Kill is a gripping thriller with a fast-paced plot and intense performances. Its suspenseful narrative keeps viewers on edge, though it occasionally relies on genre clichés. Overall, it’s an engaging watch.

Salaar

Salaar delivers high-octane action and intense drama with Prabhas leading a powerful cast. The film’s gripping storyline and stylish execution make it an engaging watch, despite some familiar tropes.
